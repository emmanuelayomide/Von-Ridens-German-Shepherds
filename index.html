Our Story

More Than a Breeder — A Lifelong Partner

As both a breeder and a professional dog trainer, my mission has always been simple: give you the best dog you've ever had. Von Ridens German Shepherds was founded on the belief that breeding excellence is about so much more than bloodlines.

Every puppy is home-raised with daily handling, gentle socialization, and the kind of early neurological stimulation that sets them up for a life of confidence and trainability. We breed for intelligence, beauty, and — above all — soul.

The Von Ridens Difference

Why Families Choose Us

We don't just breed dogs — we build relationships. Every puppy that leaves our home carries our reputation, and we take that seriously.

OFA Health Certified

Every parent dog is OFA certified for hips and elbows, and fully DM clear. We test so you don't have to guess.

Home Raised, Hand Socialized

Our puppies grow up in our home, handled daily from birth. No kennel stress — just love, play, and safe exploration.

Free Obedience Training

Every puppy purchase includes a 4-week obedience class with our professional trainer — a $300 value, included.

Championship Bloodlines

Our breeding stock holds working and conformation titles. Beauty and drive — not a compromise, a standard.

Perfect Match Placement

We spend time understanding your lifestyle before making a match. The right puppy for the right family — always.

Lifetime Breeder Support

Your relationship with us doesn't end at pickup. We're here for training questions, health advice, and life with your dog.
